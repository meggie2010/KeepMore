Hi, I'm Michael Eggie

In a world overflowing with financial advice—from social media, news, podcasts, and endless online opinions—it's easy to feel overwhelmed and unsure which path to take.

I help people cut through the noise by simplifying and automating their entire financial ecosystem—so they can eliminate money stress and focus on living the life they actually want.

I'm a personal finance coach who believes managing money shouldn't feel complicated. My approach starts with what matters most: improving your spending habits and increasing how much you save. Once that strong foundation is in place, we keep investing simple, low-cost, and effective.

My philosophy is straightforward: you don't need complex strategies to build wealth. With the right systems in place, you can spend smarter, save more, and build lasting financial security—without constantly worrying about money.
